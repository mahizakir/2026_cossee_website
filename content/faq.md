## What is the lab's motto?

COSSEE highlights four practical principles:

1. Questions drive the research.
2. Use the best open science practices available.
3. Complete the full research cycle when taking a project on.
4. Build core skills in searching, coding, writing, and collaboration.

## How does COSSEE think about open science?

Open science is woven into the lab's workflow through registrations, registered reports, published protocols, reproducible analysis, and transparent sharing practices.

## Does COSSEE emphasize equity, diversity, and inclusion?

Yes. COSSEE is committed to a safe, friendly, and accepting environment, and harassment or discrimination is not tolerated. The group frames EDI as a necessary part of meaningful open science.
