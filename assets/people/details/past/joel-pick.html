Joel Pick
Joel was a SNSF research fellow at UNSW. His work focuses predominantly on the interactions between parents and offspring, and especially on the causes and consequence of variation in parental investment in birds. I am also interested in statistical methodology, such as quantitative genetic modelling, comparative analysis and meta-analysis. He did his PhD at the University of Zurich, Switzerland, and his Masters at the University of Sheffield, UK.

Joel currently works at the University of Edinburgh, UK.
