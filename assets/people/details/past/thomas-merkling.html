As a part of Endeavour Research Fellow I was working with Lisa Schwanz and Shinichi. I am mostly interested in how environmental conditions influence reproductive decisions. During my PhD in France I investigated decisions such as sex allocation, hatching asynchrony and maternal effects in a seabird. After a postdoc at the ANU looking at lizard colouration, I came back to my first love and was doing a meta-analysis on maternal hormones and offspring sex as well as one on the influence of egg yolk hormones on offspring behaviour and fitness-related traits.

Thomas currently works at McGill University, Canada.
