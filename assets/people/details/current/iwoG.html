Iwo Gross joined COSSEE in February 2026 shortly after earning his PhD from Auburn University, Alabama, USA. In his dissertation, Iwo assessed the conservation implications of mating system dynamics in diamond-backed terrapins. Iwo work seeks to leverage empirical, simulation-based, and meta-analytical techniques to promote reproducible, evidence-based, and actionable conservation science.
