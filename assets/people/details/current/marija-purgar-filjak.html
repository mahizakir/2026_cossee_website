I joined I-DEEL in February 2026 as a Postdoctoral Scholar after finishing my PhD in marine science in my home country, Croatia. My PhD research focused on Vibrio bacteria in mariculture and on quantifying research waste in ecology. Before completing my PhD, I was a Fulbright Fellow at Emory University, where I worked on assessing open and transparent research practices across ecology and evolutionary biology literature.

My current work continues to focus on transparency in ecology and evolutionary biology, not only in research itself but across the broader research ecosystem. I’m also actively involved in the Society for Open, Reliable, and Transparent Ecology and Evolutionary Biology (SORTEE), where I currently serve as President. We recently celebrated five years of the Society and are always happy to welcome new members. You can learn more about SORTEE on our website (https://sortee.org/).
