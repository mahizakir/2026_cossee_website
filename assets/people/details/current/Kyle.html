Kyle's research focus has largely been on evidence syntheses methodologies in environmental sciences including tool/guideline development, methodological, and reporting practices, and elevating the utility of evidence syntheses to inform environmental policy-decisions. Kyle has also used evidence synthesis to explore the impacts of pesticides on aquatic ecosystems with a specific focus on fish behavioural responses. Some of his most notable first author papers have been published in Nature Sustainability, Environmental International, and Environmental Pollution.
