I am a visiting researcher and an evolutionary and behavioural ecologist from the Institute of Environmental Sciences, Jagiellonian University in Kraków, Poland. My research interests focus on the ecological and evolutionary mechanisms underlying animal adaptation to environmental change, particularly in wild bird populations. I combine long-term field studies with genetic, behavioural, and physiological data to understand reproductive strategies and life-history evolution. I have recently joined Shinichi Nakagawa’s research group at the University of Alberta, where I am learning and conducting global meta-analyses to understand reproductive strategies, particularly extra-pair copulations in birds.
