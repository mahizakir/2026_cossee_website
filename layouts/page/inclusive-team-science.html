The people, perspectives, and day-to-day collaboration behind COSSEE.

COSSEE is not just a collection of projects. It is a working research community built around evidence synthesis, open science, ecology, evolution, statistics, and collaborative tool-building. This page brings together the people who make that work possible, the different backgrounds they bring, and a glimpse of what shared research life actually looks like.

The goal is to show visitors, newcomers, and prospective members how COSSEE combines scientific depth with interdisciplinary teamwork: people working across evidence synthesis, ecology, conservation, palaeoecology, computing, machine learning, statistics, and open-science infrastructure in one connected research environment.

Scientific breadth

The team spans ecology, evolution, conservation, palaeoecology, marine science, behavioural biology, computing science, AI, and advanced statistics. That breadth matters because COSSEE's questions often need both biological insight and methodological rigor.

Shared workflows

Team science here means working with reusable code, transparent protocols, evidence-synthesis methods, and collaborative review processes so projects stay interpretable, extensible, and useful beyond one paper or one person.

Inclusive collaboration

COSSEE aims to be a place where different backgrounds strengthen the science. Respectful discussion, interdisciplinary exchange, and clear credit for contributions are part of how the lab works, not just values listed on a page.
